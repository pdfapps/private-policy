1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Information Automatically Collected

We collect certain information automatically when you use the app. This includes:

  • Device model, operating system, and system version
  • App version, screen interactions, and feature usage
  • Country (approximate), language, and time zone
  • A hashed device identifier for fraud prevention and subscription restoration
  • First app install date (used for analytics and subscription eligibility)

Purchase & Subscription Data (via RevenueCat)

When you make an in-app purchase, RevenueCat processes:

  • Transaction IDs
  • Product identifiers
  • Purchase timestamps
  • Status of active subscriptions

We do not receive or store your full payment information (Google Play handles payments directly).
